Chemistry History Month

(ft. Mendeleev and Newlands)

Re-inspiring the narrative

The Human Sovereignty, the Strongest Federation of 3XXX,

dawns on a victory for an intergalactic conflict,

yet its opponent the Kadoosh Caliphate,

on the losing side of this conflict grows restless.


In hopes of destroying humanity before they even got off the planet,

the Caliphate launches a new front charging into the rifts of time.


While the future triumphs, the past,

Without brilliant chemists leading the chemical revolution, falls.

You with your experimental time machine FCP-001,

Observing these cracks in time, follow the ruins of the past,

And attempts to restore peace to the Sovereignty.


Will you succeed?

Or will you fail, not having enough knowledge to reinspire these great scientists?

The choice is yours.

1865
